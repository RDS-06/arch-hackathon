behaviour within the next 6 months. Despite their intentions, however, they 
typically stay in this stage for long periods of time, e.g. 2 years or more. They talk 
about change, but keep putting it off. Those who substitute thinking for acting are 
called chronic contemplators. Contemplators evaluate the pros and cons of their 
behaviour as about equal; hence there is considerable ambivalence about 
changing.
