UNSTABLE ANGINA AND NON ST ELEVATION ACUTE MYOCARDIAL

6.1.1 Clinical presentation 
 
Non ST elevation  acute coronary syndrome  (unstable angina and non-ST 
elevation myocardial infarction) is defined as a change in the status of a patient’s 
angina. It includes the following presentations: (a) new-onset angina, (b) angina 
of increasing severity, frequency, duration and lesser or slower response to 
rest and/or sublingual nitrates, (c) angina occuring at rest for the first time 
and (d) post infarction angina.
