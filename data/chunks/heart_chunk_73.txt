Revascularization 
 
If cardiac catheterization is performed, either due to refractory ischaemia or as 
part of risk-stratification as described above, then revascularization should be 
considered if suitable coronary anatomy is present.  
 
The choice of percutaneous v/s surgical revascularization depends on both the 
coronary anatomy and individual patient characteristics and preferences. 
 
All patients should receive maximal preventive therapy.
