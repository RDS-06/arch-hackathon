The traditional Mediterranean diet (low saturated fat + high unsaturated fat 
especially monounsaturated) and Japanese diet (low saturated fat + high complex 
carbohydrates) are associated with the best life expectancy in the world. The 
physician’s training does not encompass aspects of dietary counseling. Therefore, 
assistance from a dietician or a specially trained nurse for dietary interviews is of 
considerable value.
