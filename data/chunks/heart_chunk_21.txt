5.4.1 Anti-anginal therapy 
 
Conditions that exacerbate and provoke angina should be considered; these 
include medications such as vasodilators, excessive thyroid replacement therapy, 
and vasoconstrictors.  Medical problems such as profound anaemia, uncontrolled 
hypertension, hyperthyroidism, and hypoxemia should also be considered.
