6.2.3 Differential diagnosis 
 
Although in the majority of cases the diagnosis of ST elevation acute MI is 
straightforward, a rapid but well thought differential diagnosis is essential. It is 
mandatory that those diseases in which thrombolysis is not only of no use but may 
even be contraindicated be excluded.
