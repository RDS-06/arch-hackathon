Rehabilitation after MI 
 
Physical activity should be progressively advanced in stable patients. Before 
discharge from the hospital patients should receive verbal and if possible written 
instructions concerning physical activities, medications, diet, and secondary 
preventive measures. 
 
Rehabilitation should concern all facets of patient’s activities: physical,
