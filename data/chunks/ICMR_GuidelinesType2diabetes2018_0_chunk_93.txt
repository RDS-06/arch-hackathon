7.2.5 Storage of insulin: 
z
z
Insulin not in use should be refrigerated in the lower compartment of the door 
of the refrigerator. It should not be kept in the freezer compartment. Ideal 
storage temperature is 2-8° C
z
z
Insulin should not be exposed to direct sunlight / heat
z
z
Excess agitation should be avoided to prevent loss of potency, clumping, 
frosting or precipitation
z
z
If refrigeration is not available, insulin should be stored in a cool place – e.g., 
in an earthenware pot of water (inside a plastic bag)
