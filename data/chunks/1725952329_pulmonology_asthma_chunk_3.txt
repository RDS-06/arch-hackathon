WHEN TO SUSPECT EXACERBATION
• Suspect if acute symptomatic worsening , or reduction in 
PEF to below 80% of personal best , while on continued 
treatment
• Take two additional puffs of the inhaler used if symptoms 
persist, and repeat if needed
• If no response after 24 hours, or symptomatic worsening, 
or further reduction in PEF, contact physician
• Physician to assess severity of exacerbation and manage 
accordingly
