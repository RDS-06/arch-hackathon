6.2.6 Post-infarction Evaluation and Secondary Prevention 
 
Coronary angiography and subsequent revascularization, either by PCI or bypass 
grafting, ultimately should be performed in patients  with recurrent angina or 
evidence of large areas of reversible ischemia on stress testing, and might be 
considered selectively in all survivors  of acute MI.
