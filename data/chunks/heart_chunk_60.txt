6.1.5 Follow-up and Secondary Prevention 
 
Many interventions have shown  utility in the secondary prevention  of ACS, 
including control of  cardiac risk factors such as  smoking, hypertension, 
hyperlipidemia, and diabetes,  regular use of aspirin, use of  beta-blockers and 
angiotensin-converting-enzyme  inhibitors in selected patients,  and adherence to 
cardioprotective diets containing  n-3-omega fatty acids,  monounsaturated fats, 
and  antioxidants . 
 
All of these interventions act by reducing the inflammatory and  thrombotic 
milieu of the  atherosclerotic vessel.
