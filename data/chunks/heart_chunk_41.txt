Studies show that the risk of death from CAD is inversely proportional to 
education.  It is therefore important for the physician to assess the patient’s 
baseline understanding of his or her condition and to appropriately educate the 
patient and his or her family on the pathology and pathophysiology of the disease, 
prognosis of condition, and treatment and risk factor reduction options.
