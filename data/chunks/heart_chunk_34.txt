CABG versus medical therapy 
Evidence from randomized clinical trials has demonstrated that coronary artery 
bypass grafts (CABG) improves mortality when compared to medical therapy for 
patients with significant left main disease, three vessel disease with left 
ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) of less than 50%, or multi-vessel disease 
with involvement of the proximal left anterior descending artery (LAD).
