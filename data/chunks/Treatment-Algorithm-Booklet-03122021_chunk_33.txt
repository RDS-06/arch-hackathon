Viral fever4,32,34
Viral pneumonia due to adenovirus, influenza A and B, 
human metapneumovirus, parainfluenza, rhinovirus, and 
cytomegalovirus. Characterized by high-grade fever, cough, 
sore throat, or myalgia
Note: Influenza-like illness is characterized by systemic signs, such as fever and 
malaise, along with the upper respiratory symptoms. The patients should be 
warned about symptoms, which indicate complications, such as breathing difficulty, 
persistent fever beyond 4–5 days, or ear pain. 
COVID-19 caused by SARS-CoV-2 is characterized by  
low-to–moderate grade continuous fever. IP: 2–4 days 
(onset of symptom average: 5–7 days). 
 Symptoms include cough, dyspnea, myalgia, headache,
