DIABETES 
 
Type 1 and type 2 diabetes are associated with a marked increase of CHD.  The 
excess cardiovascular risk associated with type 1 diabetes becomes evident after 
the age of 30.  Type 2 diabetes is associated with more profound abnormalities in 
cardiovascular risk factors than type 1 diabetes.  Even IGT is associated with a 
cardiovascular risk pattern characteristic of type 2 diabetes namely high 
triglyceride level, low HDL value, increased value of hypertension and
