RATIO OF TOTAL CHOLESTEROL TO HDL-CHOLESTEROL 
 
 
Measurement of the above ratio is also a good indicator of risk.  A total 
cholesterol/HDL ratio > 5, indicates coronary heart disease risk.

atherosclerosis.  High triglyceride levels (> 2.0 mmol) have also been
