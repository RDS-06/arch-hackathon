BLOOD PRESSURE 
 
 
Many epidemiological studies have demonstrated the importance of elevated 
blood pressure as a risk for coronary heart disease in both men and women.  The 
decision to start drug therapy depends not only on the grading of blood pressure 
level but also on the overall cardiovascular risk which calls for a proper history, 
physical examination and laboratory examinations to identify the: 
 
 
1. 
Presence of cardiovascular disease 
2. 
Coexistence of risk factors 
3. 
Incidence of target organ damage
