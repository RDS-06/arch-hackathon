REFER TO TERTIARY CARE IF
• CHF uncontrolled,
• Unstable hemodynamics
• Suspected ongoing ischemia
• Abnormal electrolytes
• Abnormal renal functions
• Structural heart disease
• Unclear etiology
MANAGEMENT AT TERTIARY HOSPITAL
1. Re-assess and conﬁrm diagnosis of HF
2. Categorize acute (< 3 months) vs chronic (> 3 months) and HFrEF
(EF 35%) vs HFpEF (EF 35-50%)
3. Optimize therapy with furosemide, ARNI, β  blockers (HFrEF,
HFMrEF, HFpEF) spironolactone and O2
4. Consider ivabradine, vericiguat and lanoxin
5. Pneumococcal and inﬂuenza vaccines
6. Investigate for etiology and manage
7. Consider non-pharmacological invasive therapy
