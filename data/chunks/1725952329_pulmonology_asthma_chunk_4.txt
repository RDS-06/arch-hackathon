SEVERE ACUTE ASTHMA (PATIENT TO BE ADMITTED)
• Inability to complete sentences, agitation, use of accessory muscles, respiratory 
rate >30/min, heart rate >110/min, pulsus paradoxus >25 mm Hg, silent chest, 
and/or room air sPo2 <92% 
• Oxygen supplementation to maintain spO2 92-95%
• Nebulized levosalbutamol/ipratropium (1.25 mg/0.5 mg) three doses at 20-minute 
interval, then 4-6 hourly or as needed
• Injection hydrocortisone 200 mg intravenously, then oral prednisolone 0.5 mg/kg 
daily for ﬁve days 
• Refer if no improvement
• Discharge only when symptoms improve, wheezing absent or signiﬁcantly
