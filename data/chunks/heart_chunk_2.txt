INTRODUCTION  
 
 
Of the cardiovascular diseases, acute myocardial infarction is the most common 
cause of death.  The incidence of acute myocardial infarction is estimated at 
around 4,000/year in Mauritius, with a total mortality rate of 30% - 40% and a 
25%-35% mortality rate before reaching the hospital.  The underlying cause in 
most cases is atherosclerotic coronary artery disease.
