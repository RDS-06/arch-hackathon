The cardinal characteristics of chest pain of acute myocardial infarction are: 
a. localisation and to a lesser  extent - radiation 
b. duration of continuous ischaemic pain 
c. response to oral nitrates 
 
Prodromal symptoms of unstable angina such as de novo angina pectoris 
during or after exercise or even at rest with increasing frequency are often 
reported (unstable angina) if properly queried.
