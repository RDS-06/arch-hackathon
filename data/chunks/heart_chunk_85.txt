Follow-up 
 
In all patients following myocardial infarction without contraindications, long-
term treatment with aspirin and β-adrenergic blockers is recommended . 
 
Recurrent angina should be treated with intravenous nitroglycerin, aspirin, 
heparin,  analgesics, and β-blockers.
