Initial Management in ALL patients  
(thrombolysis or not) 
 
• General measures: IV line, blood samples for CE, electrolytes, FBC, lipid 
profile 
• Monitoring for cardiac rhythm, vital signs. 
• If no ST elevation – repeat ECG in 1 hour (or earlier) if ongoing chest 
discomfort 
• Pain: adequate analgesia- Morphia IV 2-4 mg to be repeated in 10 min 
interval until pain is under control (or side effects +) 
• Nausea/vomiting – Primperan IV 
• Hypotension – adequate volume expansion 
• GTN s/l every 5 min (if no C/I), then infusion 
• Oxygen 2-4 l/min 
• Aspirin per os or Aspegic IV 
• Heparin - IV bolus 5000 u/kg, then 1000u/h or enoxaparin 1 mg/kg bd sk 
• Beta blockers
