Cardiac enzymes 
 
After myocardial injury and cell death, cellular enzymes are released into the 
bloodstream. The typical time course of enzyme alterations  that occur can help to 
determine the phase of MI. 
 
The most commonly used tests are creatinine kinase (CK), CK-MB, and the very 
specific troponins (troponin I or T).
